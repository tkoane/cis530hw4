Qualia Capital, Others Reportedly Eye MGM.
News Corp., Time Warner Inc. and Qualia Capital LLC might be interested in buying Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, Inc. (MGM), according to people with knowledge of the situation. The people, who declined to be identified because the talks are private, said that the firms have not examined MGMs finances and their level of interest will depend on price. MGM said last week it is weighing options, including a possible sale of the company. Creditors are hoping to get at least $2 billion, from a single buyer or by selling the assets separately, the people said. MGM might be worth between $1.6 billion and $1.7 billion. Sony Corporation may also be interested in all or part of the studio, people familiar with the situation said. Lions Gate Entertainment Corp. expressed interest in purchasing MGM at a November 12 conference in New York, and Liberty Media Corp.s Chairman, John Malone said his company would take a look.
