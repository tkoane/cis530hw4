Silver Lake Reportedly Considering Joining Consortium Bidding For FIS.
Silver Lake Partners is looking at joining the consortium bidding for Fidelity National Information Services Inc. (FIS), two sources close to the situation said. A consortium of The Blackstone Group, Private Equity Group, Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P and TPG Capital is reported to be eyeing FIS. A source close to the matter said that the potential deal could be valued at about $15 billion.
