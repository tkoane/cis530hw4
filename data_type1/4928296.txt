News Corp Reportedly Acquires Controlling Stake In Imedi.
Badri Patarkatsishvili, a Georgian tycoon has sold his controlling stake in Imedi Media Holding Group to News Corp, a television station that is part of Imedi reported. Patarkatsishvili held a 51% stake in Imedi, which includes a television station, a newspaper and a news agency. News Corp plans to hold a news conference in Tbilisi at 1300 GMT. Officials with Imedi declined to comment on the deal before the news conference.
