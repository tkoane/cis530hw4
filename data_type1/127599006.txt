News Corp Reportedly Initiates Sale Process For Myspace.
News Corp has started the process to explore the sale or spin-off of Myspace Inc., a person familiar matter said. About 20 parties have shown interest in Myspace and News Corp expects to receive more inquiries as the process develops in coming weeks. Allen & Co is reportedly advising on the process. The person stated that News Corps executives and bankers from Allen & Co will initiate discussions with interested parties in the second week of March. JNJ Mobile Inc (MocoSpace) had earlier stated that it was interested in acquiring Myspace.
