Talks Over Maritsa East III Still In Progress.
Enel SpA has announced that talks on the sale of its 73% stake in Maritsa East III are still in progress. Earlier reports had suggested that the sale of the stake for 800 million will be would be officially announced within two weeks. The company stated, "With regards to press speculation on a possible agreement for the sale of Enel Maritza East III power plant in Bulgaria to the Russian company Inter RAO UES, Enel wishes to clarify that the sale process is still ongoing and no agreement with Inter RAO UES or any other parties has been reached. Therefore no decision has been taken by Enel, pending the finalization of the process." Reports have also suggested that International Power plc, CEZ, a.s., E.ON AG, EVN AG and The AES Corporation have all shown interest in Maritsa East III.
