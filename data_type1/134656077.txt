Wipro Reportedly Joins Bidding For Misys.
Wipro Technologies Limited has joined the race to acquire the banking arm of Misys plc. An executive from Wipro, who did not want to be identified, said the firm was exploring options to stimulate growth in Banking Financial Services and Insurance (BFSI) and as part of the process, is evaluating Misys. Wipro said that as a policy, the company does not comment on market rumors and speculation. The international media reported that SunGard Capital Corp., Fidelity National Information Services, Inc. and Fiserv, Inc. are some of the frontrunners in the bidding race. Infosys, Tata Consultancy Services and HCL Technologies were also reported to be in the fray. According to a report, Misys had already opened its books to a US suitor with a bid of above ?0.450 a share. Misys was trading at ?0.4134 pence mid-day in London on June 22, 2011. The company has a market value of ?1.4 billion.
