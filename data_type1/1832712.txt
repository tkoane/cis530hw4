News Corp Interested in RCS.
According to sources, News Corp is interested in buying a stake in RCS MediaGroup SpA on its own or by joining forces with the Italian group Magiste which already has a 21 percent stake in the media group. News Corp could buy all or part of Magiste's stake, and then launch a bid for RCS. Sources added, Magiste and News Corp have been holding talks regarding the future of RCS since mid-July.
