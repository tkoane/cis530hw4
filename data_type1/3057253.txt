Murdoch Eyes Stake in Olimpia.
The CEO of News Corp., Rupert Murdoch, may buy 49 percent of Olimpia, Italian daily Il Foglio reported on July 27th, citing financial sources. Murdoch's News Corp controls BSkyB, which runs a satellite TV service in Italy called Sky Italia. Pirelli and the Benetton family group Edizione Holding would reduce their joint stake in Olimpia to 51 percent from 90 percent, the newspaper reported. Unicredit and Banca Intesa own the remaining 10 percent. Spokesmen for Pirelli and Sky Italia declined to comment. Comment from News Corp. was not immediately available.
