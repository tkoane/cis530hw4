News Corp Interested In Telecom Italia Stake.
News Corp is in the running to buy an equity stake in Telecom Italia, Italian Prime Minister Romano Prodi revealed in details of a meeting he had with Telecom Italia SpA chairman Marco Tronchetti Provera, the Wall Street Journal reported. Disclosure of details of the meeting appears aimed at heading off a possible attempt by Telecom Italia to sell its Telecom Italia Mobile (TIM) unit, according to the newspaper. In the document released late on September 13, Prodi unveiled details of conversations with Tronchetti Provera, which aim to show that the government was not aware of Telecom Italia's latest restructuring plans. The plans, announced on September 11, involve the spin off the mobile operations and the local fixed line network into separate companies.
