News Reportedly Frontrunner For Travel Channel.
News Corp is known to be the frontrunner for acquiring a 65% stake in Travel Channel Media from Cox Communications, Inc; the Financial Times reported citing insiders. The report added that Scripps Networks Interactive Inc and a consortium led by Providence Equity Partners LLC are also among the bidders. Travel Channel is likely to fetch about $800 million. News Corp's chief operating officer, Chase Carey, said the Travel Channel would fit perfectly in the company's National Geographic Channel.
