3 Telecom Cos Bid for Alltel's Local Phone Lines.
According to sources, three telecommunications companies have entered final negotiations to acquire a collection of local telephone access lines from Alltel Corp. (AT), the Arkansas-based wireless group, for as much as $10 billion. Sources said, Citizens Communications Co., CenturyTel Inc., and Valor Communications Group Inc. are competing to acquire the business, which Alltel is hoping to sell in the coming weeks. deal would most likely be structured as a "Reverse Morris Trust," in which Alltel would first spin off the access lines and subsequently have them acquired by the preferred bidder in an all-stock transaction.
