PTT In Talks To Buy ConocoPhilips' Thai Retail Operations.
PTT Public Co. Ltd. said on April 10 it is still in talks with ConocoPhillips to acquire it's Thailand retail gasoline operations. "PTT is one of the bidders. It's still in process," PTT President Prasert Bunsumpun said, declining to elaborate on details of the deal. A person close to the deal said the bidding is expected to conclude next week. Four bidders are Chevron Corp, Petronas, PTT and Shell Company of Thailand Limited, the person said. In late January, PTT Senior Executive Vice-President Anon Sirisaengtaksin said the company's interest in acquiring Conoco's service stations depends on the cost. "It doesn't mean we would pay at any price," Anon said.
