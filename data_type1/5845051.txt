JCDecaux Likely To Announce Acquisition Of News Outdoor.
Vedomosti. Reported that JCDecaux SA is likely to announce the acquisition of News Out of Home B.V. (News Outdoor). Vedomost also reported that there is a possibility of News Corp taking a stake in JCDecaux. According to source the deal may be worth about $1 billion. In August this year Kommersant had also reported about a similar deal.
