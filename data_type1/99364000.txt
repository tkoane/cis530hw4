News Corp Reportedly Mulls Selling Jamba.
News Corp. is mulling the sale of Jamba! GmbH (Jamster), Mergermarket reported, citing a source familiar with the company and venture capital investors. Zed is a likely buyer for Jamba, a banker and an industry executive said. Other potential buyers include Buongiorno, Flycell and Dada.net. Vodafone, Telefonica and Telecom Italia are all possible buyers as well, other bankers said.
