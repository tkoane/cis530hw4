News Corp. Eyes Israeli Media Companies.
According to sources, News Corp. is negotiating to acquire one of the two leading Israeli billboard companies. Representatives of the US company recently visited Israel for the purpose, and met with the Israeli companys controlling shareholders. Negotiations between News Corp. and the Israeli companies are still at the stage of examining the companies. Israeli company Cybel Holdings is handling the negotiations. Among other things, News Corp. is considering the acquisition of other media companies through Cybel. The US company has also expressed interest in advertising firms. The deal under consideration was estimated in the tens of millions of dollars. Assessments in the market are that the two media companies negotiating with News Corp. are: Maximedia Outdoor Advertising, owned by Shrem Fudim Kelner subsidiary Polar Investments and Rapid Vision Ltd., owned by Israel Land Development Company.
