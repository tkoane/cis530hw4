News Corp Optimistic About Liberty Media Talks.
News Corp. said on December 4th it was "optimistic" over reaching a deal to buy back or swap Liberty Media's shares in the company. The two companies have said they were considering swapping News Corp.'s controlling stake in DirecTV Group Inc. for the shares, although no agreements have been completed. "We would not let it drag on so long that it would affect the business," News Corp. Chief Operating Officer Peter Chernin said at a UBS media and communications conference in New York, speaking about the talks over DirecTV with Liberty Media Chairman John Malone.
