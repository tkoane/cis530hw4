News Says Has No Interest In Endemol.
News Corp. has no interest in buying Telefonicas stake in Endemol, a spokesman said on January 23. News Corp. spokesman Andrew Butcher called the speculation published in a Spanish newspaper about its interest in Endemol "nonsense." The speculation, published in La Gaceta, sent shares of Endemol up 12% in Amsterdam. A separate story in the paper speculating that Walt Disney Co. had formed an alliance with John de Mol, founder of Endemol, to buy Telefonicas stake was also shot down by a source close to Disney.
