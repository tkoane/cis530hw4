Buyers Reportedly Circling Fidelity National Information Services.
The Blackstone Group, Private Equity Group, TPG and Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P. are in talks to buy Fidelity National Information Services Inc., according to two people with knowledge of the situation. The deal is still under discussion and may not happen, said the people, who declined to be identified because the talks are private. Bloomberg reported that representatives of Blackstone, TPG and Thomas H. Lee declined to comment. It added that Marcia Danzeisen, a spokeswoman for Fidelity National, didnt return a call seeking comment. Fidelity National rose $2.68, or 10%, to $28.68 in New York Stock Exchange composite trading. That values the company at about $10.8 billion. Deutsche Bank AG, Credit Suisse Group AG and Bank of America Corp. are the banks working on the financing of the LBO, said one of the people.
