National City Mulls Sale To KeyCorp.
National City Corporation is considering a plan to sell itself to rival KeyCorp, the Wall Street Journal reported on April 2, 2008, citing sources familiar with the matter. Sources added that Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co will invest in the combined company if the transaction takes place. On April 1, 2008, National City said its board is reviewing a range of strategic alternatives and that it had hired Goldman Sachs as its advisor.
