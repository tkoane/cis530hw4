News Corp Liberty Media Deal Seen Next Week.
News Corp. could finalize a deal as early as next week to swap its controlling interest in DirecTV Group Inc and other assets for Liberty Media's $11 billion stake in News Corp., a source familiar with the matter said on December 7. Rupert Murdoch, chairman and chief executive of News Corp., and Liberty Media chairman John Malone reached a private agreement this week, the source said. The deal would involve News Corp. trading its 38.6% controlling interest in DirecTV and three regional sports networks. News Corp. also has agreed to pay $550 million in cash, the source said, confirming a report in the New York Times.
