News Corp To Reportedly Acquire Stake In Rotana.
According to a Wall Street journal report citing people familiar with the deal, News Corp. is in talks with Alwaleed bin Talal to take a stake in Rotana Media Services. A person involved with the deal told Zawya Dow Jones that News Corp. may buy a 20% interest in Rotana from Prince Alwaleed. A formal bid valuing the stake is expected this month, the person said, without giving specifics on price. A spokeswoman for Prince Alwaleed declined to comment.
