IPIC In Talks To Sell Hyundai Oilbank.
IPIC is in talks to sell part of its stake in Hyundai Oilbank Corp. to CoconoPhilips, South Korea's Yonhap news agency said on August 10. IPIC, which invests in international oil-related projects for the Abu Dhabi government, owns 70 percent of unlisted Hyundai Oilbank, South Korea's fourth-biggest refiner. Korean conglomerate Hyundai Group holds the rest.Citing unidentified Hyundai Oilbank officials, the news agency reported that IPIC was planning to sell up to a 35 percent stake to fund facility expansion at the refiner. Hyundai Oilbank was not immediately available for comment.
