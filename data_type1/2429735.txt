ConocoPhillips, Lukoil plan to buy MN stake.
Lithuanian Prime Minister Algirdas Brazauskas met with ConocoPhillips President Jim Mulva on Friday November 18 to discuss the sale of Mazeikiu Nafta. The head of ConocoPhillips said once again that the company is ready to buy a stake in Mazeikiu Nafta along with Lukoil. Mulva said that ConocoPhillips has made a concrete offer and would invest several billion dollars in Mazeikiu Nafta if the transaction is concluded.
