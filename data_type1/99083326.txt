AK Steel Shares Rise On Takeover Speculation.
The stock price of AK Steel Holding Corp recently spiked, triggered by takeover rumors. A story on Briefing.com pointed to Companhia Siderurgica Nacional (CSN), as the alleged pursuer. AK Steels representative declined to comment on "market speculation." AK Steel gained $1.56, or 6.8%, to $24.37 at 4:15 p.m. in New York Stock Exchange composite trading, the highest since January 14.
