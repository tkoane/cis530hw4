AES Reportedly Ready To Buy 49.99% Stake In Brasiliana.
Local media reported that AES Corp is ready to buy 49.99% stake in Brasiliana Energia. The Brazilian National Social and Economic Development Bank (BNDES) intend to sell 49.99% stake in Brasiliana. The deal has been valued about $6 billion. AES is also interested in the construction of a $180 million wind farm in Ceara state.
