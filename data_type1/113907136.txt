News Reportedly In Talks To Acquire Stake In Rubicon In Exchange Of FAN.
News Corp. is in advanced negotiations regarding acquiring a minority stake in Rubicon Project Inc. in exchange of FOX Audience Network Inc (FAN), a person close to the situation said. The person added that no cash will be exchanged under the terms being discussed. FAN's technology platform and a number of employees will go to Rubicon Project, the person said.
