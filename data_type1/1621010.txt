Liberty Is Eyeing News Corp. Channel.
Liberty Media Corp. may seek to buy the National Geographic Channel and is closing in on a deal to sell a voting stake back to News Corp. News Corp. owns about 67% of the National Geographic Channel's U.S. operations and 50% of the international subsidiary. Liberty Media amassed a voting stake of about 18% of News Corp. outstanding shares late last year and may use it to swap for the National Geographic Channel.
