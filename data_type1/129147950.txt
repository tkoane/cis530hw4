News Corp Reportedly In Talks With VEVO To Sell MySpace.
According to a report in Bloomberg, News Corp. is in talks with VEVO LLC to sell MySpace, Inc. The preliminary talks are on for News Corp to divest Myspace to VEVO in exchange for a stake in a new venture. News Corp. has reportedly hired Allen & Co. to evaluate offers for Myspace with a projected divestiture before the end of its fiscal year in June.
