Fastweb Denies Talks With News Corp.
Fastweb SpA on November 30 denied it has started any acquisition talk with News Corp.'s Italian unit Sky Italia SpA. Fastweb denied press reports of meetings between its founder Silvio Scaglia and News Corp.'s Rupert Murdoch. Un-sourced press reports of a potential interest of News Corp. sent Fastweb shares up over 3% to 39.45 on an overall flat market.
