AES Eyeing Privatization Of Ukrainian Energy Generating Firms.
The AES Corporation is interested in the privatization of Ukrainian energy generating companies as well as hydro-electric power facilities, Executive Director of AES Ukraine, Alfia Dzhumaeva told the InvestGazeta weekly. She said that if Ukraine launches the privatization of Donbassenergo, Tsentrenerho (Centrenergo JSC), Skhidenerho Limited, and OJSC Dniproenergo, the company will participate. Besides, she said that AES plans to continue expansion of its presence in the market of electricity distribution.
