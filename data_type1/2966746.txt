Warnaco Shares Rise on Bid Talks.
"Shares of Warnaco Group Inc. rose on June 14th at Nasdaq amidst rumors that Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. might make a bid for the company. Warnaco shares jumped $1.12, or 6.5 percent, to $18.43 during afternoon trading on the Nasdaq. Phillips-Van Heusen shares also rose, climbing 60 cents to $34.85 on the New York Stock Exchange. On June 14th, Women's Wear Daily reported unnamed sources saying that Philips-Van Heusen might consider making an offer for Warnaco, though the article noted there were no bids in the offing. Phillips-Van Heusen and Warnaco representatives declined to comment."
