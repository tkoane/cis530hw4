Energi Mega Eyeing Conocophillips' Interest In Aceh Gas Field.
PT Energi Mega Persada has said that it is interested in acquiring the Block A gas field in Aceh following ConocoPhillips' plans to sell its working interest in the block. The company said that the Block A gas field may fit its strategy of developing its gas business. ConocoPhillips decided to withdraw from Block A as it could not reach an agreement on the price of gas to be sold to fertilizer firm PT Pupuk Iskandar Muda.
