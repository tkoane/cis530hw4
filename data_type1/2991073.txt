News Corp Joins Macquarie in Bid for PCCW Assets.
News Corp Ltd is understood to have joined Macquarie Bank's $7 billion bid for the Hong Kong telecom and media assets of PCCW Ltd, the Australian newspaper reported citing unnamed sources. The newspaper, owned by News Corp, said that News Corp has taken a 'modest' stake in the Macquarie Bank consortium, possibly around 10 percent to 20 percent. A spokesman for News Ltd, News Corp's Australian division, declined to comment on the matter. Macquarie Bank also declined comment. The bank earlier said it had submitted an expression of interest in the assets. TPG Newbridge has also lodged an expression of interest for the assets.
