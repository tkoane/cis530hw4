ING, Principal, MetLife Reportedly Eyeing Citi's Pension Fund Manager.
There are rumors in the market that Citigroup, Inc. is on the look out for buyers for its Colombian pension fund manager (AFP). Sources reported that ING Groep N.V. as well as MetLife Inc and Principal Financial Group, Inc are among the possible bidders. In an emailed statement, a Citi spokesperson told BNamericas the company does not comment on market rumors or speculation.
