MidAmerican Energy May Buy FPL's Assets.
MidAmerican Energy Holdings Company may look at buying wind-power assets from FPL Group Inc, Bloomberg reported on June 1. A deal could help MidAmerican achieve a plan to add 1,001 megawatts of wind power capacity in Iowa, said Dean Crist, the company's vice president for regulatory affairs, according to Bloomberg. Bloomberg further reported that FPL offered to sell Iowa assets to keep MidAmerican from building competing farms. MidAmerican Energys officials were not immediately available for comment.
