IBM Reportedly Interested In Brocade.
As speculated, International Business Machines Corp. may be interested in acquiring Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. Brocade's shares rose 13% on the speculation. IBM and Brocade representatives declined to comment. IBM said it would spend $20 billion on acquisitions by 2015 and it has spent more than $12 billion on 23 analytics-related acquisitions over the past four years.
