Indonesian Government Backs Pertamina's Bid To Take Over Aceh Gas Field.
The Indonesian government will support PT PERTAMINA's bid to take over the operation of the A Block gas field in Nanggroe Aceh Darussalam to help overcome the gas crisis in the province, State Enterprises Minister Sugiharto said. Previously, the two foreign firms holding the concession rights to the gas field, CONOCOPHILLIPS and EXXONMOBIL, were reported to be planning to sell 50 per cent of their shares.
