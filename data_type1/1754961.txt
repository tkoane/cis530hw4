AAF Stake Draws Interest From Exxonmobil, Conocophillips.
According to sources, ExxonMobil Oil Indonesia Inc and ConocoPhillips have expressed interest in buying a 40 percent stake in fertilizer firm PT Asean Aceh Fertilizer. The source said both companies have notified the government of their interest. The 40 percent stake in AAF is estimated to be worth 70 billion rupiah ($4.09 million). The stake belongs to the governments of Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore and the Philippines. Indonesia owns the remaining 60 percent.
