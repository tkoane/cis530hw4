News Corp. Reportedly To Receive Bids For MySpace This Week.
News Corp. is expected to receive bids for MySpace, Inc. by the end of this week, a person familiar with the matter said. MySpace is expected to get around 5 or more bids valuing it at more than $100 million, according to this person. Redscout Ventures, Criterion Capital Partners, LLC, Thomas H. Lee Partners, L.P., Tencent Holdings Ltd. and Chris De Wolfe are expected to bid.
