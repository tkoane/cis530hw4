Phillips Van Heusen Says in Tommy Talks with Apax.
Phillips Van Heusen Corp. has said that it is in preliminary talks with Apax Partners on how they can collaborate on Apax's agreement to buy clothing maker Tommy Hilfiger. Apax is a Phillips Van Heusen shareholder. Apax announced on Dec 23rd that it agreed to buy Tommy Hilfiger for around $1.6 billion.
