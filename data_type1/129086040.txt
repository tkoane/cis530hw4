ConocoPhillips May Reportedly Divest Properties In Kazakhstan.
ConocoPhillips seeks to divest stake in oil and gas properties in Kazakhstan, Kazakhstan Today reported. ConocoPhillips is considering divest its stake in oil and gas properties in countries including Libya and Kazakhstan as part of planned asset sales, according to Argus oil analyst Phil Weiss, Reuters reported. "Selective country exits are also possible. Candidates would be Vietnam, Libya, Algeria, Kazakhstan and Nigeria," Weiss wrote following a meeting with ConocoPhillip's chief financial officer and head of investor relations. Shares of the company rose 45 cents to $80.15.
