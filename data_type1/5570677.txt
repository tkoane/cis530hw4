Endesa Makes Offer For ESB.
According to a report in La Gaceta de los Negocios, Endesa SA has made an offer to Electricity Supply Board (ESB). The report added that Electricity Supply Board is valued at around 450 million. Electricity Board which is in the process of being privatized has also received offers from four consortiums. The report added that, Bord Gais Eireann, Star Capital Partners Limited, Viridian Group plc and AES, are all interested in ECB. A decision regarding the sale is expected to be made by the Irish Government in Mid July.
