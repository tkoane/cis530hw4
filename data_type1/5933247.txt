OVL Eyeing 8 Iraqi Oil, Gas Blocks.
ONGC Videsh Ltd (OVL) is looking at the possibility to make a bid for the eight oil and gas blocks that are being auctioned by Iraq. OVL is one of the companies along with Royal Dutch Shell Plc, ExxonMobil Corp, Chevron Corp, ConocoPhillips, BP Plc, Total SA and China National Petroleum Corporation (CNPC), short-listed by the Iraqi government for the auction. The bidding is probably going to take place in the next few months and the blocks may be offered by June in 2009. The oil minister of Iraq, Hussein al-Shahristani met officials of the 35 short-listed companies in London. Our team is in London meeting the Iraq officials. Once our team is back we will take a call on which blocks to bid for, said an official with OVL.
