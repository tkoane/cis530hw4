ConocoPhillips Receives Interests For Syncrude Canada.
ConocoPhillips said that it has received strong interest from potential buyers interested in acquiring its 9% stake in Syncrude Canada Ltd. The firm said that it plans to sell the stake as part of $10 billion in dispositions over two years. Clayton Reasor, the company's vice-president of corporate affairs, said, We started the sales process for our Syncrude interests, and you will see quite a bit of interest on this asset. Canadian Oil Sands Trust is seen as the logical buyer. It said that it is interested in increasing its ownership position in Syncrude Canada.
