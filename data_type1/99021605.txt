Phillips-Van Heusen Reportedly In Talks With Apax To Buy Tommy Hilfiger.
New York Post reported, citing unidentified persons that Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. is in exclusive talks to acquire Tommy Hilfiger Corporation from Apax Partners Worldwide LLP for as much as $4 billion. The report added that Phillips-Van Heusen may close the transaction within two weeks. It was also reported that Apax had planned to list the company on Amsterdams Euronext stock exchange. Under the proposed sale, Apax would retain about 30% in Hilfiger, the report added. The Post reported that Tommy Hilfiger and Apax declined to comment and Phillips-Van Heusen did not respond to a request for comment.
