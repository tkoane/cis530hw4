Oracle Not Eyeing Brocade.
The CEO of Oracle Corp., Larry Ellison wants his company to sell complete systems that combine hardware and software. We are not buying Brocade Communications Systems, Inc, Ellison said on October 7 at the companys annual shareholders meeting. The declaration came two days after The Wall Street Journal reported that Brocade was seeking a buyer, and that Oracle, along with Hewlett-Packard was one of the companies that might be interested.
