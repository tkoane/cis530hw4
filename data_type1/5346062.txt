National City Denies Rumors Of Nova Scotias Offer.
National City Corp. declined in early trading after the Wall Street Journal reported Bank of Nova Scotia had made an offer to invest in the bank. Corsair Capital LLC is considering making a bid, while KeyCorp and Fifth Third Bancorp have already submitted offers, the Journal said, citing unidentified people familiar with the matter. Also, according to the report, Warburg Pincus LLC has withdrawn from the bidding.
