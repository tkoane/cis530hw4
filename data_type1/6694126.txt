News Corp Reportedly Close To Selling Weekly Standard To Anschutz.
The Los Angeles Times reported citing people close the development that News Corp is close to a deal to divest Weekly Standard to Philip Anschutz. The report stated: Weekly Standard has been a pet political project of News Corp Chairman Rupert Murdoch, but now that he owns the Wall Street Journal that reaches a bigger number of people, Murdoch may not really need the Weekly Standard. Reuters reported that a News Corp representative denied commenting on the issue.
