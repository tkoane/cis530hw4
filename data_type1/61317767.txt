Unitedhealth Reportedly In Talks With Health Net.
Unitedhealth Group, Inc. might be in talks to acquire U.S. Northeast operations of Health Net, Inc., sources familiar with the situation said. The sources cautioned that the discussions still could fall apart without an agreement. The negotiations do not include Health Net's business in Arizona, for which it is still seeking a buyer. It is believed that Health Net has been conducting a strategic review of its operations in the Northeastern United States and Arizona for several months. Reuters reported that Health Net and UnitedHealth declined to comment.
