News Corp. Picks Up Stake in TV Puls.
News Corp. has bought a roughly 25 percent stake in TV Puls, Polish newspapers reported on June 28th. The station is owned by a Franciscan brotherhood. "The brotherhood has invited News Corp to work with us as an investor," newspapers quoted TV Puls chief executive Father Zdzislaw Dzido as saying. Papers said that News Corp. had bought a 50 percent stake in TV Investycje, which owns just under 49 percent of TV Puls. Dzido declined to give any details of the value of the deal. Earlier, papers had reported the company was seeking around 100 million zlotys ($30.87 million) in investment in the first year.
