News Corp Close To Buying Stake In Rotana Media.
News Corp. is in final negotiations with Prince Alwaleed bin Talal regarding buying a stake in Rotana Media Services, Nezar Nagro, president of Rotana Media Services said on December 15, 2009. We are in the final discussions with News Corp. for the deal and the results will be out by the last week of January, Nezar Nagro, said. The president however declined to reveal how big a stake Rotana Media plans to sell. In September the Wall Street Journal said the stake being discussed was 20%, citing people familiar with the deal.
