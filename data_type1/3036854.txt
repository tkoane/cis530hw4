Mergermarket Down to Three Final Bidders.
It was reported that the sale of Mergermarket is down to three final bidders, namely McGraw-Hill Cos., General Electric Co. and Pearson PLC. The board of the data company will meet to discuss the offers, which range from $240 million to $300 million. It is understood that all three offers envisage current management remaining with the company. Mergermarket was put on the block March 2006, by Beringea LLC and NewMedia Spark PLC, and initially drew some 14 bids including private equity and trade buyers. Representatives for General Electric and Pearson weren't available to comment. A spokeswoman for McGraw-Hill said it is company policy not to comment on market speculation.
