News Corp Reportedly Eyeing Telecom Italia.
News Corp. is reportedly in talks to acquire a stake in Telecom Italia SpA. Dow Jones reported quoting La Repubblica, that Rupert Murdoch, CEO of News Corp. has held talks with Cesar Alierta, chairman of Telefonica SA. Telefonica holds a stake in Telecom Italia through Telco S.p.A. News Corp. is looking to expand in the Italian telecom sector and is already represented in Italy through its unit Sky Italia S.r.l. The deal could however face opposition from Mediaset SpA, which is a competitor of Sky Italia.
