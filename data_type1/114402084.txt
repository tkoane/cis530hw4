Korea Electric Power Eyes Covantas Stake In Quezon Power Facility.
Korea Electric Power Corp. (KEPCO) is eyeing Covanta Holding Corporations 26% stake in 460-megawatt Quezon Power facility, banking sources said. Covanta has placed its stake in the auction block for another round after a sale fell more than a year back. The other bidders are The AES Corporation and Electricity Generating Public Co. Ltd. KEPCO is also eyeing BG Group plc's 40% stake in First Gas power plants.
