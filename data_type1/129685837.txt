News Corp. Reportedly In Talks with Tencent, De Wolfe And Others For MySpace Sale.
News Corp. might lay off more employees of MySpace, Inc. in conjunction with a deal to sell MySpace, according to people familiar with the matter. News Corp. has had talks with Tencent Holding Ltd., VEVO LLC, Chris DeWolfe, The Gores Group LLC and Criterion Capital Partners, LLC, according to people familiar with the matter. News Corp. also has reached out to venture capital firms about a MySpace deal. Executives have explored scenarios where News Corp. would combine MySpace with another site in exchange for cash and equity in the merged company. Tencent and Vevo couldn't be reached for comment by Wall Street Journal.
