LUKOIL May Join Hands With Conoco For Iraq Oil Fields Bid.
As per company sources, LUKOIL Oil Company is interested in bidding for at least two of the fields Iraq is offering. We are interested in both the West Qurna and the Rumaila oilfields, a source said. The source added that, LUKOIL may combine with ConocoPhillips in order to bid for the fields.
