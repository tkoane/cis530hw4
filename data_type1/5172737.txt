News Corp Eyes Stake In Yahoo.
Yahoo Inc. is said to be discussing a possible partnership with News Corp., in order to fend Microsoft Corporation's bid, a person familiar with the situation said. The terms of the proposed joint venture haven't been worked out, according to the person, who didn't want to be identified because the talks are considered confidential. Wall Street Journal and TechCrunch reported that News Corp wants to merge its 'My Space.com' and other Internet assets into Yahoo. News Corp and a private equity firm would buy considerable stakes in Yahoo, to boost its market value above Microsoft's initial bid of $44.6 billion, or $31 per share. A Yahoo spokesman said the company continues to "carefully and thoroughly" evaluate alternatives that will enrich its long-term shareholders.
