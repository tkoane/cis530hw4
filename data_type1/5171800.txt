Bebo Reportedly Acquired For $1 Billion.
According to Techcrunch a high level source has said that Bebo, Inc. along with its advisor Allen & Co has been in talks, with a number of potential buyers. The source emphasized that the company signed a deal on February 11, 2008, to be acquired for $1 billion. The source could not reveal the buyer but it is speculated that potential acquirer is either Google or Microsoft. Other potential buyers, including News Corp. and Yahoo are understood to have backed out due to price. There are other potential buyers as well which include CBS, Viacom, and Comcast.
