EDF Reportedly In Race For OGK-1 Stake.
According to a La Tribune report, EDF has emerged as a candidate to take a stake in OGK-1 as part of the ongoing privatization of the sector in Russia. SEU is planning to sell 75% of OGK-1. It currently holds 91.7%. The paper said that EDF filed a request with Russia's antimonopoly agency adding that EvroSibEnergo and TransNeftService are also competing for the stake and have done the same. Separately Vedomosti reported that RWE AG and AES Corp are also in the running.
