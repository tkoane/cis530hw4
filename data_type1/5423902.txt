Cablevision To Make $650 Million Bid For Newsday.
According to a report in the Wall Street Journal, Cablevision Systems Corp. (CVC) is understood to be looking at the possibility of making a $650 million bid for Tribune Co.'s Newsday. Citing people close to the situation, the report said that Cablevision's bid, which was likely to come within the next 48 hours, would give the company the edge over rival bidders News Corp. and Mortimer Zuckerman.
