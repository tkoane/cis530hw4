Yukos Ready to Hold Mazeikiu Nafta Talks with 4 Potential Buyers.
Yukos is ready to hold negotiations on selling its 53.7% share in Mazeikiu Nafta with four potential buyers. Yukos said that negotiations are quite possible with TNK-BP, Lukoil, and ConocoPhillips if they make higher bids. Meanwhile, Kazakhstan's KazMunaiGaz and Poland's PKN-Orlen have made the highest bids.
