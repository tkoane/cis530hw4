Group 4 Securicor, Others Express Interest In Sellafield.
Group 4 Securicor PLC has emerged as a bidder in the British government's plan to privatize Sellafield, according to a report. Group 4 is one of seven groups that have submitted pre-qualification questionnaires to the Nuclear Decommissioning Authority (NDA), the Daily Telegraph said. Other consortia that have approached the NDA about buying the plant include a group consisting of Amec PLC, Areva and the Washington Group, another including Serco Group PLC and Bechtel and BWX Technologies, the Telegraph said. Babcock International Group PLC is also on the list, as is Fluor Corp, EnergySolutions and CH2M Hill.
