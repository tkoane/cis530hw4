CIC Reportedly Eyes Stake In AES.
China Investment Corporation (CIC) is in discussions to acquire a minority stake in the AES Corporation, the Wall Street Journal reported, citing people close to the matter. The report stated that the potential deal is aimed at building an alliance between AES and CIC and the discussions may result in CIC acquiring a significant stake in AES. The Wall Street Journal reported that a joint venture between CIC and AES is also under discussion, in which CIC would provide capital to support AES in developing power plants around the globe. The report added that people close to the matter indicated that the talks are at a sensitive stage and might not produce a deal. Reuters reported that a CIC media official said it would not comment on rumors concerning its investment activities and AES could not be reached for comment.
