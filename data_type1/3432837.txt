Petronas, PTT And Indian Firm To Bid For Conoco Fuel Stations In Thailand.
Petroliam Nasional Bhd (Petronas), PTT and an unidentified Indian company will bid for Conoco's 147 service stations in Thailand, Bernama newswire reported. PTT executive vice-president Anon Sirisaengthaksin said Conoco is expected to finalize the deal with one of the three bidders next month. The Bangkok Post reported that Conoco is pulling out due to long-term losses.
